Currently, I am a Cultural Observation Researcher for the 15th "Sign of Future" program at the Market and User Research Department (i-MUR) of Tencent IEG, and I have a keen interest in user emotional engagement and its dynamics. Personally, I am fond of anime girl bands (such as 《ガールズバンドクライ》, 《BanG Dream! It's MyGO!!!!!》, and 《けいおん！》) and light yuri anime (such as 《超かぐや姫》 and 《ゆるキャン△》). In the summer of 2026, I will join the Zhang Integrated Ecology Lab (ZIEL, <wenyuanzhang.com>) at the School of Life Sciences, Nanjing University, as a visiting student under the guidance of Prof. Wenyuan Zhang, to further study AI for Ecology, with a particular focus on the application of causal inference and computer vision in biodiversity conservation, as well as modeling complex ecosystems for quantitative research and prediction.

## Research Interests
My research interests span across mathematics (mathematical logic and probability theory), philosophy (metaphysics and scholasticism), and statistics, with a specific focus on causal inference. I am also deeply interested in brain and neuroscience, particularly computational neuroscience, as well as the quantitative modeling of complex systems, which I apply to both ecological studies and broader interdisciplinary research.
